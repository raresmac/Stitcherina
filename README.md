### How to use:

1.  **Set your grid size:** Choose how many stitches wide and high you want your masterpiece to be.
2.  **Upload your photo:** Pick a photo or a previous Stitcherina save file.
3.  **Adjust the Merge:** Slide the tolerance bar until your palette feels just right.
4.  **Generate:** Hit the button and let the magic happen!
5.  **Stitch away:** Click the blocks as you go to stay organized.
